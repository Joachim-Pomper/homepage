Teaching assistent

During my time as a student I had multiple jobs as teaching assistant, where I was tutoring classes, correcting homework and exams, as well as organizing lecture materials. I really like to communicate science to other people. In my opinion you only understand a topic fully if you can explain it to another person. Thus teaching was and is a great experience for me, because I often need to rethink what I thought I already understood. Currently I am a teaching assistant for Advanced Quantum Mechanics I.
